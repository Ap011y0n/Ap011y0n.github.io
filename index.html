PORTFOLIO

Albert Garcia Belerda



Hi, allow me to introduce myself. My name is Albert, I'm a game developer from Terrassa, Spain.

I've been playing games and videogames all my life, and started developing interest in it's creation process at a very young age.

At some point I took it one step further and started a university degree focused on the whole process, where I developed interests on programming and game design , and learnt other useful knowledge about user experience, modeling and animation. On the last years, I've been developing a strong interest in graphics programming, and collaborated with some indie studios developing graphical effects and also done some research on the topic myself.

This page is a compilation of most of my projects, please feel free to explore them!



Videogames

I've worked in different projects with different teams ranging from 2 to 30 members, mostly as a programmer.

Game and level design

I've analysed and also created some levels of my own, so I could improve my knowledge about frameworks like the ccst, the flow of a multiplayer combat map, the player guidance, etc.

Engines and tools

During this time, I learned to use a lot of engines such as Unity and Unreal 4, and also some useful softwares and tool like blender, maya, Wwise and many more. I also developed some engines and tools of my own, such as a physics engine, procedural generators, AI and data analysis tools.

Research projects

I also did some research projects with academic purposes, mainly on procedural narrative, where I realized an extensive investigation on the matter, but I also worked on group movement patterns, and AI agents.
